Sunset Float

Perfect for families and first-timers, this gentle float down a calm stretch of the river offers beautiful scenery and a relaxing experience as the sun sets. A great way to unwind and connect with nature.

Big Mallard Canyon

Our most popular half-day trip! Experience the thrill of Class III rapids mixed with scenic, calm stretches. This adventure is perfect for those looking for a bit of excitement without being too extreme.

Los Dientes del Diablo

For the ultimate thrill-seeker! This full-day trip tackles the challenging Class IV and V rapids of the "Devil's Teeth." Previous rafting experience is recommended for this intense and rewarding adventure.
