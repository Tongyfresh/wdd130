Favorite City

Of all the cities I've had the pleasure of visiting, Chicago holds a special place in my heart. It's not just the stunning architecture or the incredible food scene that captivates me, but the memories woven into its streets. My wife and I honeymooned there, and the city became the backdrop to the start of our life together. For my wife, who is from Iowa, Chicago has always been a beacon of urban excitement and a familiar getaway. We find ourselves drawn back time and again, not just for the attractions, but for the city's infectious energy and the warm, welcoming vibe that makes it feel like a second home.
